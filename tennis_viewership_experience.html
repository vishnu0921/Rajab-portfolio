OVERVIEW

A digital experience to make
“what’s happening on your
screen is as exciting as the action
on the court”.

French Tennis Grand Slam is an annual tournament held in Paris. It is one of the four tournaments that make up the Grand Slam of tennis. It has started over a century ago and over time has emerged to be the premier tennis tournament and the only among the Grand Slam tournaments to be played on clay. The client team approached us intending to provide fans, journalists, players, and coaches with a new digital-driven experience.

PROBLEMS

Initial findings highlighted several
problems areas such as:

01

Spectators were only able to view current
points in play, with no provision to review
prior point data.

02

No real-time way for spectators to view
faulty serves, except for the match data
projected on television.

03

Other than basic match statistics, there were
not any insightful data points generated that
could be shared with the media, spectators,
players, and their coaches.

04

There was no social engagement for
spectators to receive updates about the
Grand Slam tournaments.

How did we help resolve the
problem

Sports data visualization, is a visual communication tool that involves the use of data and advanced statistics to measure team performance and gain a competitive sports advantage. Our innovations like MatchBeats, MatchBeats++, Stats, CourtVision, and Rally Analysis are dynamically integrated into Match-centre to elevate the fan experience.

MatchBeats

MatchBeats and MatchBeats++ elevate the live depiction of a match providing fans with a set-wise game flow with filters to spot key trends.

Stats

Stats is a unique way of displaying statistics, where disproportionate influence is calculated based on the volume and cruciality of the match.

Rally Analysis

Rally Analysis provides an interesting picture to spectators and analysts wherein they can analyze the outcome of a match based on player performance across various rally lengths.

CourtVision

CourtVision provides an in-depth match analysis by overlaying match statistics on a 2D court.

Players Portal

Players Portal is a video creation software that suggests AI-generated video clips for every tennis statistic along with its AI score, which does numerous real-time checks for a particular shot.

Slam Leaderboards

Slam Leaderboards is another novel way of ranking tennis players using the core statistical data collected from the Grand Slam Tournament and AI ranking.

AI Shot of the day

AI shot of the day is an AI solution that recommends the best tennis shots from each day for the media team at Grand Slam Tournament to compile and publish on their platforms.

AI-Assisted Journalism

AI-Assisted Journalism is a feature targeted for sports journalists to augment their capability of creating match reports through readily available match insights.

Stat of the day

Stat of the day presents a list of more than 15 unique stats on each day to the media team personal to choose and publish the best one on various social channels.

AI Highlights

AI Highlights is a new digital offering for the media team at the Grand Slam Tournament who automates a highly creative task and helps generate and publish the highlights in 2mins of match completion.

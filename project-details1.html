OVERVIEW

A hybrid tablet application for
enhancing sales experience.

Machinery Manufacturer client who is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and distribution of agricultural solutions with its headquarters in Georgia, United States. They also do parts, machinery service, finance as a service to their customers. The client team approached us to create an application for their sales team to keep track of their customers needs. In this project, I have collaborated with Brilliant Basics the design and innovation studio based out in the UK and It was great working with them.

We have gathered information about the users and their process of ordering products in the existing system. We synthesize the data we have collected from the stakeholders and extract the insights, which helps us move forward in this design process.

How did we help resolve the
problem

A hybrid tablet application that runs out of all the OS platforms, combines elements of both native apps and web applications. This made the salesperson to mobile freely. It helps the user to keep track of the customers from the initial stage to the final one. we have implemented the ideas like Offline entry, Events schedule, Customer Opportunity, Stock search, Lead capture, etc.

Offline entry

Offline entry helps the salesperson to save the entry offline when the internet is not available and lets the data sync online when he connects back to it.

Events

Events help the salesperson and the customer to schedule a meeting for all the services provided by the company.

Customer Opportunity

Customer Opportunity lets the salesperson keep track of the stages followed to pitch for a new customer till the product being sold.

Stock search

Stock search helps the salesperson know of the dealer's inventory and being notified of it.

Lead capture

Lead capture lets the salesperson collect key information for the identification of purchasing intent.

CONCLUSION

The process of tracking the customers, Scheduling meetings, Searching stocks, Capturing new leads, Recording sales, etc. using the legacy system was hectic for the salesperson but this product shows that with this mobility design solution we can solve this issue easily and make the users work effective.
